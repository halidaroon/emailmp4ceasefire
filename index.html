Guide on mass-emailing Parliament to demand a ceasefire
  1. Make a copy of this spreadsheet into your Google Drive.

    Click "File" (top menu bar)>> Click "Make a Copy" >> Click the green button (Make a Copy)

  2. Allow Mail Merge to access your inbox.

    Click "Mail Merge" (top menu bar)>> [Authorization Required] Click "OK" >> Choose the account you want to send the emails from >> [Google hasn't verified this App] Click "Advanced" >> Click Go to "Gmail/Sheets mail merge (unsafe)"

  3. Draft an email in Gmail

    Feel free to use this email template, but I highly encourage you to write your own email with a personal touch! Do not remove the text with curly brackets (eg: {{Minister Proper}}) because these will be used to personalise each email.

    Dear {{Minister Proper}},

    I hope this letter finds you well. My name is [YOUR NAME], and I am writing to you as a concerned Singaporean deeply troubled by the escalating conflict in Gaza. The recent developments have not only caused immense human suffering but have also heightened tensions in the region.

    I am aware of the complexity of the geopolitical situation and the challenges faced by governments in addressing such conflicts. However, I believe that diplomatic efforts and international collaboration are crucial in achieving a lasting solution that ensures the well-being of civilians on all sides.

    The current situation in Gaza demands urgent attention and intervention to prevent further loss of life and alleviate the suffering of innocent civilians. I am writing to respectfully urge the Singaporean government, under your esteemed leadership, to advocate for an immediate ceasefire in Gaza, and for a cease in all purchases of Israeli weapons. A cessation of hostilities will provide an opportunity for dialogue and diplomatic efforts to address the root causes of the conflict.

    As a nation known for its commitment to peace, stability, and diplomacy, Singapore holds a unique position to influence positive change. Your position as {{Position}} and the diplomatic prowess of the Singaporean government could play a pivotal role in promoting dialogue and facilitating negotiations between the parties involved. Furthermore, as one of the top 10 purchasers of weapons from Israel, Singapore is capable of creating strong disincentives to the continuation of this conflict.

    I understand the complexities of international relations, but I believe that a strong and principled stance in favor of a ceasefire will resonate globally and contribute to the broader efforts for a peaceful resolution to the conflict in Gaza.

    I sincerely hope that you will consider this appeal with the utmost urgency and compassion. Your intervention can make a significant difference in preventing further loss of life and fostering an environment conducive to diplomatic negotiations.

    Thank you for your attention to this matter, and I remain hopeful that your leadership will contribute to the promotion of peace and stability in the region.

    Yours sincerely,

    [YOUR NAME]

    Make sure to write a compelling subject line in your email and mark it as important!

  4. Send out the email via Mail Merge

    Click "Mail Merge (top menu bar)" >> Click "Send Emails" >> Copy and paste the subject line of your draft email >> Click "OK" >> Do not exit the application until the dialog box [Running script] finishes >> Refresh the spreadsheet and check if all emails are sent by looking in the "Email sent" column. >> Delete rows which are sent and repeat with unsent rows until all are sent!
